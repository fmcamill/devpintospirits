Business Summary

Pinto Spirits is an independent Australian spirits representation and brand development company based in Melbourne, Victoria. It works with a carefully selected portfolio of premium Australian spirits producers, helping them grow through on-trade strategy, venue placement, hospitality relationships, and brand education.

Pinto Spirits is not a liquor retailer, online bottle shop, wholesaler, or alcohol marketplace. It does not sell alcohol directly to the public.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pinto Spirits?

Pinto Spirits is an independent Australian spirits representation and brand development company based in Melbourne. It helps premium local producers grow through on-trade strategy, venue placement, and bartender education.

Does Pinto Spirits sell alcohol?

No. Pinto Spirits is not a retailer, bottle shop, or online store. It represents and develops spirits brands; it does not sell alcohol to the public.

Who founded Pinto Spirits?

Pinto Spirits was founded by Paul Slater, who has more than twenty years of experience in the Australian spirits industry and serves as a judge for the Australian International Spirits Awards.

Which brands does Pinto Spirits represent?
